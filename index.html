Delicious Local Cuisine

Interesting food and tourist place to be visit

Place: Pasar Payang

Offers a wide variety of goods, including fresh produce, local handicrafts, textiles, and traditional Malay food.

Place: China Town

A historic area with colorful buildings, traditional shops, and cultural landmarks, offering local food and heritage sites.

Place: Pulau Perhentian

a tropical paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant marine life, making it a top spot for snorkeling and diving.

Food: Sotong celup tepung

Featuring battered, deep-fried fish. Similar dishes include sotong (squid) and udang (shrimp) celup tepung.

Food: Keropok lekor

Snack made from fish and flour, shaped into long rolls and deep-fried, served with chili sauce.

Food: Laksam

Dish of thick rice noodles served with a rich, creamy fish-based gravy, often garnished with fresh herbs.

Discover the Beauty of Malaysia

Drawbridge Terengganu
Experience the unique architectural marvel and stunning views of Terengganu's Drawbridge.
China Town
Explore the vibrant streets of Chinatown, rich with cultural heritage, delicious food, and historic architecture that reflects Malaysia's multicultural identity.
Pulau Perhentian
Marvel at the breathtaking views from the skybridge of the tallest twin towers in the world.
Pasar Payang
Immerse yourself in local culture at Pasar Payang, a bustling market where you can find traditional crafts, fresh produce, and delicious street food.
Museum Negeri Terengganu
Discover the rich history and cultural heritage of Terengganu at the state museum, featuring fascinating exhibits and artifacts.
Crystal mosque
Visit the stunning Crystal Mosque, renowned for its breathtaking architecture and serene surroundings, offering a glimpse into Islamic art and design.
